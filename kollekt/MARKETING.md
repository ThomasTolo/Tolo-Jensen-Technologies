# Kollekt — Page-by-Page Marketing Copy

> Short captions for each screenshot, placed next to the image on the website.
> One headline + 2–3 lines max per screen.

---

## 1) Log In

**Welcome home.**

Your shared flat, finally in one place. Log in with email or Apple — and get back to what matters.

---

## 2) Register

**Shared living starts here.**

Create your account in seconds. No setup fees, no landlord login. Just your name and a password.

---

## 3) Onboarding — Create a Home

**Build your home from scratch.**

Name it, add your rooms, and let Kollekt do the rest. Chores auto-rotate every Monday so no one gets stuck with the same job twice. More effort = more XP.

---

## 4) Join with Code

**Got a code? You're in.**

Ask a roommate for the 6-letter household code and join instantly. The task rotation reshuffles the moment you arrive — no waiting for Monday.

---

## 5) Profile & Household Code

**Your life in the house, at a glance.**

Set your status to Active or Away, pick your colour, and share your household code with new roommates. Your XP, rank, and level are always right there.

---

## 6) Home

**Good morning. Here's your house.**

A real-time snapshot of everything — who's done their tasks, what the house vibe score is, your balance, and what's on this week. The home screen that actually earns its name.

---

## 7) Tasks

**Whose turn is it? Now everyone knows.**

Weekly tasks assigned fairly, automatically. Earn XP for every one you check off. See what's left, what's done, and who's slacking — all in one view.

---

## 8) Calendar

**One calendar. Four roommates. Zero confusion.**

Add events, see the whole house schedule, and subscribe directly to your phone's calendar. No more "I didn't know there was a thing tonight."

---

## 9) Chat

**The group chat that actually does stuff.**

Polls, laundry coordination, GIFs, reactions, kudos — it's the household chat with superpowers. Switch between the house thread and private DMs with a swipe.

---

## 10) Chat — Reply & React

**Long-press to reply. Long-press to react.**

Threading keeps conversations clean. React with any emoji, reply inline, or just send a laughing face. Real-time, every time.

---

## 11) Chat — Action Bar

**More than just text.**

Send photos, drop a GIF, start a poll, flag a laundry run, or give someone a kudo — all from the same message bar. Communication that fits how you actually live.

---

## 12) Economy

**Money made fair.**

Log shared expenses, split them across whoever was involved, and see exactly who owes what. One tap to settle up via Vipps, MobilePay, or PayPal. No spreadsheets. No awkward conversations.

---

## 13) Pant Tracker

**Your bottle deposit, tracked together.**

Log every bottle you collect and watch the savings grow. Set a goal — like a house dinner at NOK 1,000 — and hit it as a team.

---

## 14) Leaderboard

**Who's the best roomie this month?**

A live podium based on XP earned from tasks. Set a custom prize for the winner — pizza, a free pass on dishes, whatever fits your house. Resets when you want it to.

---

## 15) Games — Tonight's Pick

**Game night, sorted.**

Kollekt picks a game for tonight and puts it front and centre. Browse by Classic, Party, or Cards — every game is built-in, no app switching needed.

---

## 16) Games — The Full Lineup

**13 games. Zero prep.**

Dice, Spin the Wheel, 100 Questions, Chain Story, Mexican, and more. Each card shows the rules, players needed, and how long it takes. Just hit Start.

---

## 17) Profile — House Rules & Settings

**Your house. Your rules.**

Set quiet hours, write the house rules everyone has to acknowledge, and flag violations anonymously if things go wrong. Switch the app language between English, Norwegian, Swedish, or Danish.

---

## 18) Dark Mode

**Easy on the eyes at midnight.**

Full dark mode. Because chore reminders at 11 PM shouldn't blind you.

---

## 19) Girl Mode *(Rose Theme)*

**Make it yours.**

Kollekt comes in multiple themes. Switch to Rose for something a little warmer. Your home, your aesthetic.

---
